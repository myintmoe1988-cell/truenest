About ED Visa

ED Visa (Education Visa) allows foreigners to stay in Thailand long-term while studying a course such as language programs.

At True Nest BKK Service, we provide Language School ED Visa services. Depending on the course, classes can be online or in-person.

You can choose visa durations from 4 months up to 15 months, and you can continue studying another course to extend your stay.

This visa is also useful for applying for third-country visas from embassies inside Thailand.

Since full-time attendance is not always required, you can work freelance, remote jobs, or part-time jobs while staying in Thailand.

Requirements

  • Name
  • Gender
  • Date of Birth
  • Nationality
  • Passport Number
  • Email Address
  • Telephone Number
  • Myanmar Address
  • Mother / Father Name
  • Thailand Contact Person (Name, Phone, Address)
  • Certificate of Education
  • Passport Bio Page
  • Passport Visa Page
  • E-Visa (if available)
